Unimog 406 Doka

Mercedes-Benz Unimog - 406 Doka Tug

This 406 Doka is an excellent example of a round body double cab Unimog. Aside from the bottom side of the rear doors is got very little rust as the body had treatment put on it.

It's a versatile truck that handles everything from tugging planes to camping in the forest.

It will cruise about 40-45mph.

There is no other vehicle that matches the Unimog for it's dependability and capability.

Specs

  • Year: 1974
  • Make: Merceds-Benz Unimog
  • Model: 406 Doka Tug
  • Engine: 5.7-liter OM352 Diesel Inline-Six
  • Transmission: 6 forward and 2 reverse manual, torque converter, three locking differentials

MJ Notes

The double cab (Doka) and short wheel-base (Tug) of this Mog make it a blast to drive around. PTO on front, hydraulics both front and rear it is also ready to do a bit of work around the farm in desired. Another interesting feature is the torque converter. This means you can put it in 6th gear, let off the clutch and it will slowly come up to speed. (not the recommended way to drive)
