Unimog 406 Doka

Mercedes-Benz Unimog - 406 Doka Tug - $60k

G-wagon, G-wagen, Geländewagen however you want to say it they are the 2nd longest running production vehicle from Mercedes-Benz (behind Unimog with 75years). These trucks have always been made in Graz, Austria however in some countries they were branded Puch to create a brand association with the Pinzgauer which was renown for it’s off-road capabilities.

There are three main G designations the 460 - original, 461 - military variants, 463 - New blingy ones. This is 461 230 GE produced for the Swiss military. The Swiss took good care of their vehicles and stored them indoors but did have a habit of walking on the hoods to get out. (thus most have dented hoods)

This has a 4 speed automatic transmission, selectable low and high four wheel drive along with a rear locker. It’s powered by a gasoline inline 4 cylinder producing 125HP. (M102 engine)
It will cruise 60mph fine even reaching 65/70 when pushed. The truck is incredibly utilitarian and configurable by nature. It shines with the rear seats installed carrying 8 people and towing a raft trailer with the sides up so people can see but still have sun protection. Pull the rear top off and you have a pick up truck with a rear swing door. Pull off all the tops and have a totally open experience safari experience!
